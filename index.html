About Jem Foundation

JUSCO EDUCATION MISSION FOUNDATION, a registered trust was established in 2008 by Jamshedpur Utilities and Services Company to ensure the growth and sustainability of the undertaken educational initiatives in the area where company operates. Ever since its inception, the trust has earnestly executed its mandate of ameliorating the quality of life of the communities through education.
